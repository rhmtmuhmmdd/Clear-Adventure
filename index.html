Our Mission

We believe that every journey should leave the environment as clear as we found it.

Our mission is to promote conscious adventures ‚Äî where respect for nature, safety, and sustainability come first. We explore mountains, forests, and wild spaces with a simple principle: leave nothing behind but footprints, take nothing but memories.

Through every step, we aim to inspire adventurers to travel lightly, act responsibly, and protect the natural beauty for future generations.

About CLEAR.ADV

Explorers who believe responsibility and adventure are inseparable.

Our Story

We Explore.
We Protect.

CLEAR.ADV is a community driven by a shared love for mountains, forests, and wild landscapes. We are explorers who value responsibility as much as adventure. Every expedition we organize is built on teamwork, preparation, and environmental awareness.

Founded in 2024 in South Sulawesi, we began as a small circle of passionate hikers who saw that exploration needed a more conscious approach ‚Äî trails littered, cultures unrespected, and unprepared climbers putting themselves and others at risk.

So we built something different. CLEAR.ADV is not just a hiking club ‚Äî it is a movement. A commitment to exploring the world's wild places with eyes wide open and footprints as light as possible.

Exploration Guidelines

  • Leave no trash ‚Äî carry out everything you carry in
  • Respect local culture and customs of host communities
  • Safety first ‚Äî know your limits, never exceed them recklessly
  • Travel prepared with proper gear and emergency plans
üéí

Equipment Checklist

  • Hiking boots with proper ankle support and grip
  • Rain jacket and waterproof outer layer
  • Headlamp with extra batteries (always carry a spare)
  • Sufficient water supply and purification tablets
  • Emergency kit ‚Äî first aid, whistle, space blanket
