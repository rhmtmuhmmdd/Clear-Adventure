Who We Are

About CLEAR.ADV

CLEAR.ADV is a community driven by a shared love for mountains, forests, and wild landscapes. We are explorers who value responsibility as much as adventure. Every expedition we organize is built on teamwork, preparation, and environmental awareness.

Founded in 2024 in South Sulawesi, we began as a small group of passionate hikers who felt that exploration needed a more conscious approach. We saw too many trails littered, too many cultures disrespected, and too many unprepared climbers putting themselves and others at risk.

So we built something different. CLEAR.ADV is not just a hiking club ‚Äî it is a movement. A commitment to exploring the world's wild places with eyes wide open and footprints as light as possible.

We welcome adventurers from all skill levels, backgrounds, and professions. What unites our members is not experience, but intention: the desire to explore meaningfully and leave things better than they were found.

Our expeditions are documented carefully ‚Äî not for social media glory, but to build a living archive of responsible adventure. We hope these records inspire others to follow in our footsteps, carefully and with deep respect.

If these values resonate with you, we invite you to reach out. There is always room for one more on the trail.
